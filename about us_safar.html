Who We Are

Established in 2015, Safar Adventures was founded by avid travelers who wanted to share their passion for exploration with fellow adventurers. What began as a personal quest for discovery has evolved into a full-fledged travel agency dedicated to delivering exceptional experiences.

Our Vision and Mission

Vision

Our vision at Safar is to ignite a sense of wonder and discovery through immersive journeys that showcase the rich cultural tapestry, diverse landscapes, and hidden treasures of India. We aspire to redefine domestic travel by curating authentic and sustainable experiences that deepen connections with local communities and celebrate India's heritage. Our goal is to inspire travelers to explore the soul of India, fostering a deeper appreciation for our country's beauty and diversity

Mission

At Safar, our mission is to inspire wanderlust and create meaningful connections through personalized travel experiences. We believe in responsible tourism, sustainability, and fostering cultural appreciation.
