Gangotri

Discover the spiritual allure of Gangotri amidst the breathtaking Himalayan landscapes.

Gangotri is a sacred site, known for its spiritual significance and beautiful surroundings. It offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage, making it a must-visit destination.

Ooty

Explore the serene beauty of Ooty with its lush tea gardens and picturesque landscapes.

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu, known for its pleasant climate, stunning views, and charming colonial architecture.

Allepey

Experience the backwaters of Alleppey, on a relaxing houseboat cruise through scenic waterways.

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is famous for its network of canals, backwaters, and lagoons. A houseboat cruise here offers a tranquil and picturesque experience.
