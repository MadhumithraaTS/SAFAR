PICTURESQUE ANDAMAN

ENCHANTING ISLANDS AND SECLUDED SPOTS


Nature is at its best versions in the exotic islands of Andaman and Nicobar, as lesser-explored landmasses are blessed with numerous pristine beaches, impressive lined up coconut palms, astonishing backwaters, dense rainforests and above all that the turquoise blue ocean waters. All of these are inevitably going to impress you with their indescribable aesthetic beauty, even if you had been choosing the hills or any busy top-notch urban spaces to make your holidays the best. your travel preferences are also, going to be altered once you get to know more about it. It’s only been a few years that the very concept of tourism had surfaced in this splendid Union territory positioned to the north-eastern coast of the Indian mainland, beyond the Bay of Bengal.

ITINERARY


5 Days/4 Night₹20000 per head




DAY 1


Explore the historic Cellular Jail and relax at Corbyn's Cove Beach, followed by a captivating light and sound show at the jail in the evening.



DAY 2


Catch an early morning ferry to the beautiful Havelock Island. Spend the afternoon at the famous Radhanagar Beach, known for its pristine sands and clear waters. Enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and beach activities before checking into your resort. Relax and soak in the tranquil island atmosphere for the evening.



DAY 3


Dive into adventure with a morning scuba diving or snorkeling session at Elephant Beach, where you can explore vibrant marine life and corals. After an exhilarating water adventure, visit the serene Kalapathar Beach in the afternoon, perfect for a peaceful walk and scenic photography.



DAY 4


Take a morning ferry to the tranquil Neil Island, arriving in time to relax at the picturesque Laxmanpur Beach. In the afternoon, visit the fascinating Natural Bridge, also known as Howrah Bridge, during low tide to see this unique rock formation. Enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the island and soaking in its serene ambiance before retiring to your hotel.



DAY 5


Spend the morning at Bharatpur Beach, return to Port Blair in the afternoon, and conclude your trip with souvenir shopping before departure.
